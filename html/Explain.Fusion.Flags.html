Decoding Fusion flags

This utility makes it easy to identify what are the properties of a SV event based on its Fusion flag value, or conversely, to find what the Fusion Flag value would be for a given combination of properties.

To decode a given Fusion flag value, just enter the number in the field below. The encoded properties will be listed under Summary below, to the right.
